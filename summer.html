Magic Mind

This site is designed to provide meditation aids. So when you are stuck at home or in your office and you need to relieve stress, you can gaze at your monitor and it will help to empty your mind. You can then either meditate or just relax and reduce your stress..

Beaches

Close your eyes and imagine yourself on a beach. With the warm sun on your face. The air feels moist and embracing. You can hear the sound of seagulls calling and the rhythmic lapping of the waves on the shore. Your feel wonderfully relaxed and free. Small and yet at peace next to the ocean. Choose a picture that you like and click on it to enlarge it. Look at the photo and imagine yourself on the beach.



Sunsets

Close your eyes and imagine that it is the end of the day. You are at peace with your accomplishments for the day. You can sit and relax for a while until you need to get ready for dinner. This is a time to be alone and relax. Your work is done for the day and now it is just quiet time. You watch the sun, a wondrous ball of red energy gently sink down the sky. Look at the photo and imagine yourself relaxing as dusk fills the air.



Lakes

Image you are sitting on the edge of a clam lake. The water gently laps around your ankles. The air feels so fresh against your skin. Wherever you look beautiful nature surrounds you. All you can hear is silence and the gentle chirping of a bird. You feel completely at peace at this moment. You feel like you have found your perfect moment in the world. Close your eyes and imagine this.



Mountains and valleys

Imagine you are looking into the distance as a magnificent mountain is bathed in sunshine. It's size is inspiring. Below you are beautiful green valleys of filled with luscious green trees and plants. You feel at peace with the world around you. There is so much beauty and natural rhythm to the world. You feel in awe of the harmony of the world around you and a deep sense of peace. Close your eyes and imagine this.



Perspectives

Imagine you are sitting in a quiet place. The view stretches out into the distance. The sky is a deep blue and the air is clear and feels fresh against your skin. You are feeling at peace with the world. You feel your stress float away and a deep calm come over your body as you stare at the beautiful scenery before you. You let your thoughts drift away and your mind empty.
